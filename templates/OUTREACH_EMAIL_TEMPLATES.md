# Outreach Email Templates

Neutral, professional templates for common outreach scenarios.

**Usage rules**
- Keep messages short.
- One clear ask per message.
- Remove anything you cannot support with evidence.
- Prefer specific time options.

---

## 1) LinkedIn Connection Request (no note, or short note)
**Short note (300 chars)**
> Hi <Name> — I’m a <lane/role> based in <location>. I’m interested in <team/domain> work at <Company>. Would you be open to connecting?

**If you have a shared anchor**
> Hi <Name> — I saw your work on <topic/team>. I’m a <role> working on <relevant area>. Would you be open to connecting?

---

## 2) Informational Interview Ask (after connecting)
**Subject:** Quick question about <Team/Role> at <Company>

> Hi <Name>,
>
> Thanks for connecting. I’m exploring <lane/role> opportunities and <Company> is on my target list. If you’re open to it, I’d appreciate 15–20 minutes to ask a few questions about your team’s work and what you see as the hiring priorities.
>
> If that works, I can do <2–3 time options> (PT). If easier, I can also send questions by message.
>
> Best regards,
> Rongjun

---

## 3) Recruiter Reply (initial inbound)
**Subject:** Re: <Role Title> — next steps

> Hi <Recruiter Name>,
>
> Thank you for reaching out. The role aligns with the work I’ve been doing in <lane/area>, especially <2–3 relevant keywords>.
>
> I’m open to an initial call to confirm scope, team structure, and location/remote expectations. If helpful, please share a few time options and the job description.
>
> Best regards,
> Rongjun

---

## 4) Cold Email to Hiring Manager (direct)
**Subject:** <Role Title> — <one-line value hook>

> Hi <Name>,
>
> I’m reaching out because I’m interested in the <Role Title> opening on your team. I’ve worked on <2–3 relevant areas>, including <proof keyword 1>, <proof keyword 2>, and <proof keyword 3>.
>
> If you are the right contact, I’d like to share a short overview and confirm whether my background matches what you need most right now.
>
> Would you be open to a brief call this week? I’m available <2–3 time options> (PT).
>
> Best regards,
> Rongjun
> <LinkedIn>
> <Portfolio/GitHub>

---

> Why I think it fits:
> - <Fit bullet 1>
> - <Fit bullet 2>
>
> If yes, I can send a 3–4 bullet summary you can forward, plus my resume and the job link.

> Rongjun

## 8) Short “Forwardable” Summary (for referrals)
> Rongjun is a <lane/role> with background in <2–3 areas>. Recent work includes <proof 1>, <proof 2>. Strong fit for <Role Title> due to <reason 1> and <reason 2>. Links: <LinkedIn>, <Portfolio>.

---

## 9) Templates for recruiters: clarifying questions (copy/paste)
Use these as a checklist; only ask what matters.

> Before we schedule, could you confirm:
> - level and core success criteria for the first 90 days
> - team structure (IC vs leadership expectations)
> - location/remote policy and on-call (if any)
> - compensation range and interview process outline

---

## 10) Placeholders
- `<LinkedIn>`: your profile URL
- `<Portfolio/GitHub>`: your chosen public artifact link
- Keep signatures consistent across templates.
