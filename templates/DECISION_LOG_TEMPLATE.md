# Decision Log Template

Use this template in: `career-architecture/logs/decisions/YYYY-MM.md`.

**When to log a decision**
- Changes your lane, constraints, or operating mode
- Commits you to an experiment or a public artifact
- Creates a rule you will want to enforce later
